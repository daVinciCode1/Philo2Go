About

Philo2Go was founded based on a need for academic resources for philosophy that are not in book form for a change. Our team is constituted of philosophy enthusiasts that want to share their passion and knowledge in an easily accessible data base. Be it for your Philosophy course at university, or to satisfy your curiousity for the deep questions in life, we are here to help.

We are just at the start of our journey to make philosophy more accessible to everyone and hence will stick to weekly articles for now.
